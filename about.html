Brought together...

by the powers that be. We are a group of misfit programers making the world better one website at a time. Spanning the country, we’ve coordinated across time-zones to fight against houseplant deaths. We envision a world where greenery can thrive in every home, without resorting to silk plants or hiring certified plant waterers. Our current mission is to train up an army of global citizens that can say with confidence, “Why yes, I do water my ficus only when it needs it.” Eventually, if we have our way, not only will everyone have lush surroundings and plenty of silent green friends, but the compassion they train in the process will bring us one step closer to world peace & harmony. After all, it’s hard to be a curmudgeon when you’re cooing to your cacti every day.
